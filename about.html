About This Project

Learn Ge’ez is a student-created project dedicated to preserving and teaching the ancient Ethiopian language, Ge’ez. Built with beginners in mind, this site is meant for anyone — Ethiopians and non-Ethiopians alike — who want to explore the roots of Ethiopia’s linguistic heritage.

The idea behind this project came from a deep curiosity about ancient languages and a desire to make cultural knowledge more accessible in the digital age. Ge’ez is more than a language; it is a vessel of history, tradition, and sacred literature. Through this platform, I hope to bring its beauty and structure closer to those who are eager to learn but unsure where to start.

All content and design were created with careful attention to clarity, authenticity, and simplicity — using only basic HTML, CSS, and a genuine passion for learning. This project also reflects my interest in history, philology, and the intersection of language and identity.

Thank you for visiting. I hope this site sparks curiosity, inspires learning, and revives something timeless.
