The Ge'ez Alphabet

The Ge'ez alphabet took its writing system from the Sabaean system. Most of the letters are similar or nearly similar to the Sabaean alphabet. Two more letters are taken from the Greek alphabet. Look at the comparison between the Sabaean and the Ge'ez letters below.

The Ge'ez script includes over 200 characters. Among these, 26 base characters evolve into 7 forms depending on the vowel, making up 182 forms. The remaining 20 are labiovelars, some with only 4 forms. These forms express a wide variety of vowel sounds and syllabic combinations in the language.

Labiovelars / ሕጹጻን ፊደላት

“ሕጹጽ” implies something incomplete. These 20 characters are considered special because some of them do not follow the standard seven-form pattern. Four have only four forms and serve unique phonetic roles in Ge'ez orthography.

Amharic Alphabet Derivations

The Amharic alphabet adapted the Ge'ez alphabet, taking its shape from it:

  • ሸ took its form from ሰ
  • ቸ took its form from ተ
  • ኘ took its form from ነ
  • ኸ took its form from ከ
  • ዠ took its form from ዘ
  • ጀ took its form from ደ
  • ጨ took its form from ጠ

Meanings of the Letters

Meanings of the Letters

The Ethiopian Orthodox Church assigns unique, prayer-inspired meanings to each letter.

ሀ — The Father's life was before the world

Ge'ez: ብሂል ሀልዎቱ ለአብ እም ቅድመ ዓለም፡፡
Amharic: የአብ አኗኗሩ ከዓለም በፊት ነው፡፡
English: The Father's life was before the world.

ለ — Christ was incarnated from the Virgin Mary

Ge'ez: ብሂል ለብሰ ሥጋ እምድንግል፡፡
Amharic: ክርስቶስ ከድንግል ማርያም ሥጋን ለበሰ፡፡
English: Christ was incarnated from the Virgin Mary.

ሐ — Christ suffered, died, and was buried

Ge'ez: ብሂል ሐመ ወሞተ ወተቀብረ፡፡
Amharic: ክርስቶስ ታመመ፣ ሞተ፣ ተቀበረ፡፡
English: Christ suffered, died, and was buried.

መ — God's work is wonderful

Ge'ez: ብሂል መንክር ግብሩ ለእግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: የእግዚአብሔር ሥራው ድንቅ ነው፡፡
English: God's work is wonderful.

ሠ — The Lord was born (manifested) in human form

Ge'ez: ብሂል ሠረቀ በሥጋ እምድንግል፡፡
Amharic: ጌታ በሥጋ ተወለደ (ተገለጠ)፡፡
English: The Lord was born (manifested) in human form.

ረ — The earth is established by His word

Ge'ez: ብሂል ረግዓ ሰማይ ወምድር፡፡
Amharic: ምድር በቃሉ ረጋች (ጸናች)፡፡
English: The earth is established by His word.

ሰ — Our Lord became man

Ge'ez: ብሂል ሰብአ ኮነ እግዚእነ፡፡
Amharic: ጌታችን ሰው ሆነ፡፡
English: Our Lord became man.

ቀ — It was originally a word

Ge'ez: ብሂል ቀዳሚሁ ቃል ውእቱ፡፡
Amharic: በመጀመሪያ ቃል ነበር፡፡
English: It was originally a word.

በ — Our Lord came down to us in His humility

Ge'ez: ብሂል በትኅትናሁ ወረደ እግዚእነ፡፡
Amharic: ጌታችን በትሕትናው ወደኛ ወረደ፡፡
English: Our Lord came down to us in His humility.

ተ — Our Lord became man

Ge'ez: ብሂል ተሰብአ ወተሰገወ፡፡
Amharic: ጌታችን ሰው ሆነ፡፡
English: Our Lord became man.

ኀ — God is powerful

Ge'ez: ብሂል ኀያል እግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔር ኀያል ነው፡፡
English: God is powerful.

ነ — Our Lord has borne our infirmities and carried our sicknesses

Ge'ez: ብሂል ነሥአ ደዌነ ወፆረ ሕማመነ፡፡
Amharic: ጌታችን ደዌያችንን ያዘልን ሕመማችንን ተሸከመልን፡፡
English: Our Lord has borne our infirmities and carried our sicknesses.

አ — I thank God

Ge'ez: ብሂል አአኲቶ ወእሴብሖ ለእግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔርን አመሰግነዋለሁ፡፡
English: I thank God.

ከ — God is omnipotent

Ge'ez: ብሂል ከሃሊ እግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔር ሁሉን ቻይ ነው፡፡
English: God is omnipotent.

ወ — He came down from the heavens

Ge'ez: ብሂል ወረደ እምሰማያት፡፡
Amharic: ከሰማያት ወረደ፡፡
English: He came down from the heavens.

ዐ — God is great

Ge'ez: ብሂል ዐቢይ እግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔር ታላቅ ነው፡፡
English: God is great.

ዘ — God is the Ruler of all things

Ge'ez: ብሂል ዘኲሎ ይእኅዝ እግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔር ሁሉን የሚገዛ ነው፡፡
English: God is the Ruler of all things.

የ — The right hand of God has made power

Ge'ez: ብሂል የማነ እግዚአብሔር ገብረት ኀይለ፡፡
Amharic: የእግዚአብሔር ቀኝ ኀይልን አደረገች፡፡
English: The right hand of God has made power.

ደ — He united His divinity with our flesh

Ge'ez: ብሂል ደመረ መለኮቶ ምስለ ሥጋነ፡፡
Amharic: መለኮቱን ከሥጋችን ጋር አንድ አደረገ፡፡
English: He united His divinity with our flesh.

ገ — He is the One who created the earth and the heavens

Ge'ez: ብሂል ገባሬ ሰማያት ወምድር፡፡
Amharic: ምድርና ሰማያትን የፈጠረ ነው፡፡
English: He is the One who created the earth and the heavens.

ጠ — God is wise

Ge'ez: ብሂል ጠቢብ እግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔር ጠቢብ ነው፡፡
English: God is wise.

ጰ — The Paraclete is the Spirit of truth

Ge'ez: ብሂል ጰራቅሊጦስ መንፈሰ ጽድቅ፡፡
Amharic: ጰራቅሊጦስ የእውነት መንፈስ ነው፡፡
English: The Paraclete is the Spirit of truth.

ጸ — God is grace and truth

Ge'ez: ብሂል ጸጋ ወጽድቅ እግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔር ጸጋና እውነት ነው፡፡
English: God is grace and truth.

ፀ — God is the sun of truth

Ge'ez: ብሂል ፀሐየ ጽድቅ እግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: እግዚአብሔር የእውነት ፀሐይ ነው፡፡
English: God is the sun of truth.

ፈ — He created the world with His wisdom

Ge'ez: ብሂል ፈጠረ ዓለመ በጥበቡ፡፡
Amharic: በጥበቡ ዓለምን ፈጠረ፡፡
English: He created the world with His wisdom.

ፐ — The name of God is Papael

Ge'ez: ብሂል ፓፓኤል ስሙ ለእግዚአብሔር፡፡
Amharic: የእግዚአብሔር ስሙ ፓፓኤል ነው፡፡
English: The name of God is Papael.
