Welcome to Learn Ge'ez!

This site is created for anyone still seeking, still curious about the roots of Ethiopia. you may be an Ethiopian or not, but if you're interested in the Ge’ez language — you're in the right place.

You can find out about the history of the language, train with beginner-level lessons, simple explanations, and resources to help you explore a language that history tried to bury, but couldn't erase.

These old stories and this old language hold something worth listening to. Through forgotten texts, through ancient symbols, wisdom echoes.

You don’t need to know much to begin. All you need is curiosity and persistence.

Let’s explore Ge’ez together.

Why Learn Ge’ez?

Ge’ez is Ethiopia’s ancient tongue. But it’s also her archive — the one language in which her golden history was encoded. To learn Ge’ez is to walk through the roots of a civilization that wrote it on stone and parchment. In every character, there's a legacy. In every word, a voice from the past. Let’s awaken Ge’ez!
